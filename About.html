CompartmentalizeR

The future of modeling is here

CompartmentalizeR is an R-Shiny app for the interactive creation of compartmental models.

It allows you to:

  • Express your models graphically.
  • Use an intuitive drag-and-click interface.
  • Start creating models without requiring coding skills.
  • Connect with R, one of the top statistical packages.
  • Adjust data to your model (coming soon)
